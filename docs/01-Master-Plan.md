# 01 - ASAP Services Website Master Plan

This document defines the vision, milestones, architecture and success
criteria for the ASAP Auto Electrics Services Website.

## Goals

-   Maximise Google Ads ROI
-   Dominate Local SEO
-   Generate workshop bookings
-   Keep ecommerce separate at shop.asapauto.com.au


Version: 1.0
Project: ASAP Auto Electrics Services Website
Platform: Next.js 15 (App Router)
Language: TypeScript
Hosting: Vercel
Status: Reusable service-page system implemented; ten priority service pages complete and awaiting review

# 1. Project Vision

Build Australia's best Auto Electrical services website.

Primary goals:

1. Maximise Google Ads conversion rate.
2. Dominate Local SEO throughout Melbourne's Eastern Suburbs.
3. Generate workshop bookings.
4. Showcase the professionalism of ASAP Auto Electrics.
5. Keep ecommerce completely separate from service enquiries.

This is NOT an ecommerce website.

Products will eventually move to:

shop.asapauto.com.au

The main domain will become a dedicated service website.

# 5. Geographic Position

IMPORTANT

ASAP DOES NOT PROVIDE MOBILE SERVICE.

Customers bring vehicles to our Ringwood workshop.

Every location page MUST state this clearly.

Correct wording:

"Our fully equipped workshop is located in Ringwood and proudly services customers from Melbourne's Eastern Suburbs."

Never imply technicians travel to customer homes.
